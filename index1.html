The Pakistan Telecommunication Authority (PTA) is planning to regulate the use of Virtual Private Networks (VPNs) in the country. This move aims to control unauthorized internet usage and enhance cybersecurity. Users will need to register their VPNs with the authority. The PTA believes this step will help curb illegal activities and ensure a secure digital environment. Critics, however, raise concerns about privacy and internet freedom.
